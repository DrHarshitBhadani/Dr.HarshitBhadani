About the clinic
Take a tour of the clinic.
Pictures of clinic. Equiped with latest and sophisticated equipment.
Located near PNB Salt Lake City Sector-III, Kolkata, the clinic is well equipped with latest and sophisticated equipment which are required to provide a world-class medical treatment. The materials used are of the best quality and from the best of the manufacturers operating around the globe. Your health is our top-most priority. The clinic is at the ground floor, is air-conditioned, has a nice, clean lobby (waiting area). There are free vehicle-parking spaces available for outside the clinic. Wheelchairs are also available. The premises is shared with some of the leading Doctors from Kolkata and Hyderabad. The premises also houses a Diagnostic Centre, a Pharmacy, a fitness centre, and a cafe.
